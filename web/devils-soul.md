---
title: "Devils Soul"
template: recipe.mustache
slug: devils-soul
time: "2 minutes"
makes: "1 serving"
cocktail: |
  # Devils Soul
  glass: coupe
  ice: none
  method: stir, strain

  spine:
    1.5  rye
    0.5  mezcal
    0.5  averna
    0.25 aperol
    0.25 st-germain
ingredients:
  - 1.5oz Rye
  - 0.5oz Mezcal
  - 0.5oz Averna
  - 0.25oz Aperol
  - 0.25oz St. Germain
steps:
  - Combine ingredients with ice in a mixing glass and stir until chilled
  - Strain into a coupe glass)
  - Express orange rind over the top
notes:
  - I first heard about this cocktail on reddit, and was surprised by how much I liked the flavor combination.
