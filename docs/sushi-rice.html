Sushi Rice

info

  • TIME 30 minutes
  • MAKES 2 servings

ingredients

  • Dry sushi rice

steps

  1. Rinse 1.5 cups rice until water runs clear
  2. Combine the 1.5 cups rinsed rice with 2 cups water in a saucepan and soak for 15 minutes
  3. Bring to a soft boil, then reduce heat to low, cover
  4. Simmer for 20 minutes **(or until moisture is absorbed)**
  5. Remove from heat and let stand covered for 10 minutes
  6. Fluff and serve

notes

  • I have occasionally not done the initial 15 minute soak with seemingly minimal impact on the final product.
  • If you want it to actually taste like sushi rice, add some rice vinegar/ mirin
