Devils Soul

info

  • TIME 2 minutes
  • MAKES 1 serving

ingredients

  • 1.5oz Rye
  • 0.5oz Mezcal
  • 0.5oz Averna
  • 0.25oz Aperol
  • 0.25oz St. Germain

steps

  1. Combine ingredients with ice in a mixing glass and stir until chilled
  2. Strain into a coupe glass)
  3. Express orange rind over the top

notes

  • I first heard about this cocktail on reddit, and was surprised by how much I liked the flavor combination.
