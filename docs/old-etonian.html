Old Etonian

info

  • TIME 2 minutes
  • MAKES 1 servings

ingredients

  • 1.5oz Gin
  • 1.5oz Lillet Blanc
  • 0.25oz Crème de Noyaux
  • 2 dashes Orange Bitters
  • Orange peel

steps

  1. Prechill a coupe glass in the freezer
  2. Add the gin, lillet, crème de noyaux, and bitters to a shaker with ice and shake until cold
  3. Fine strain into the chilled glass
  4. Express an orange peel over the cocktail and use as a garnish

notes

  • This is basically a sweet variant of a wet martini, and I've found it to be tasty both shaken or stirred.
