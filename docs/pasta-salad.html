Pasta Salad

info

  • TIME 30 minutes
  • MAKES 2 servings

ingredients

  • Pasta **(something like fusilli or farfalle works well)**
  • Onion
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Capers
  • Parsley
  • Canned tuna
  • Lemon
  • Maldon salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

steps

  1. Cook the pasta [al dente](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Al_dente) and then drizzle with olive oil in a bowl
  2. Halve cherry tomatoes and add
  3. Add chopped capers
  4. Add chopped parsley
  5. Add finely diced onion
  6. Add 1 can of tuna **(in oil ideally)**
  7. Add a squeeze of lemon juice, Maldon salt, and pepper to taste

notes

  • This template works great with canned tuna, but is also amenable to other protein options, be that a different tinned fish, or cubed charcuterie like salami or chorizo.
  • I also occasionally enjoy adding some chopped olives if I have them on hand.

Based On
