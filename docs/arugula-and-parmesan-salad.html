Arugula and Parmesan Salad

info

  • TIME 5 minutes
  • MAKES 1 servings

ingredients

  • Arugula
  • Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Salt/ Pepper

steps

  1. Put an appropriate amount of arugula in a bowl
  2. Shave strips of the parmesan over the arugula using a vegetable peeler
  3. Add a glug of olive oil and balsamic vinegar
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  5. Mix and serve

notes

  • Super quick and easy salad that works well as an accompaniment to a variety of main courses.
  • The vinegar can easily be substituted for a different acid, such as lemon juice, or an alternate vinegar
