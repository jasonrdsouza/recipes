Burkett Old Fashioned

info

  • TIME 2 minutes
  • MAKES 1 serving

ingredients

  • 2oz Rye Whiskey **(Redemption High Rye)**
  • 0.5oz Amaro Averna
  • 0.25oz Demerara Simple Syrup
  • 3 Maraschino Cherries
  • 2 dashes Black Walnut Bitters
  • 1 Barspoon of Maraschino Cherry juice

steps

  1. Add the rye, amaro, simple syrup, cherry juice, and bitters to a mixing glass with ice and stir until cold
  2. Strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube
  3. Garnish with the 3 cherries and serve

notes

  • The original recipe this cocktail is based on didn't call for the maraschino cherry juice, but after workshopping it with Nick and Andrew, we found that it added a nice depth of flavor.
  • We also tried substituting bourbon for the rye whiskey, as well as a split base of bourbon and scotch to give it a smokier flavor, but ended up settling on all rye as the tastiest version.
  • There was much debate as to whether the shape of the ice cube mattered, with resident cocktail expert Nisha convinced that a large cube was preferable to the large spheres that Nick was using. This debate was never resolved.
